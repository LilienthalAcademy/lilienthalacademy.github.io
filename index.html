Our Story

Lilienthal Academy is our family’s homeschool adventure. Rooted in love for learning, we’ve built an environment where every question is welcome, and every child’s unique path is respected.

Curriculum

  • Core: Reading, Math (Saxon & Singapore), History (Story of the World)
  • Science: Nature walks, experiments, Apologia
  • Enrichment: Art, Music, Coding, Spanish
  • Faith: Bible studies woven through all subjects
